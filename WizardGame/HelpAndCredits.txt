Move with the WASD keys, pause with "/". Make your way through the tower while fighting off evil monsters using your wizardly skills!


Basic Movement W, A, S, D
Attack I, J, K, L

Health potions have a random chance of appearing after the death of an enemy. 
They are shown as a "+" symbol. Walk over it to regain your health.

Commands "/"
/ Help - view help
/ Credits - view credits
/ Resume - resumes game
/ Quit - quits game
